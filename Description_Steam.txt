[h1] What is this mod? [/h1]

This mod aims to bring another level to your skill and game knowledge.

While playing, every in-game month the stats of every country are logged.
These stats include:

[list]
    [*] manpower
    [*] nr of armies
    [*] amount of ships
    [*] fuel
    [*] amount of light tank armies
    [*] medium tank armies
    [*] heavy tank armies
[/list]

Also available on [url=https://mods.paradoxplaza.com/mods/26454/Any] Paradox Mods [/url] !

[hr][/hr]

[h1] Instructions [/h1]

Follow the instructions [url=https://github.com/c-jaenicke/hoi4-game-analysis#instructions] here [/url].

[olist]
   [*] Subscribe to this mod
   [*] Download the [url=https://github.com/c-jaenicke/hoi4-game-analysis/releases] analyse.exe [/url]
   [*] Drag the "game_logging.log", located in [code] Documents/Paradox Interactive/Hearts of Iron IV/logs/variable_dumps/ [/code] onto the analyse.exe
   [*] Import the generated game_analyse.csv into a spreadsheet program
[/olist]

Check the [url=https://github.com/c-jaenicke/hoi4-game-analysis/wiki] Wiki [/url] on how to do that, and process the information.

[b] If windows blocks the file, its an error, click "more" and "execute" [/b]
If you dont trust me, you can check the code yourself on GitHub!
